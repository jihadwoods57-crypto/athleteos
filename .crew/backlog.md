# Founder Product Backlog

Judgment improvements the crew found but will **not** ship on its own — taste / product / psychology /
coach-parent value / education / growth / simplification calls that only the founder makes. The crew ranks
them; you decide. Refreshed at the start and end of each run.

The top of the list answers the crew's mission question: **what single change would most improve OnStandard
tomorrow?** (It may be a product move, not a bug.)

## Format
```
### #<rank> · <pillar> · <title>   (impact 1-5, effort s/m/l)
- Why: <why it matters to the athlete / coach / parent>
- Evidence: <screen · tap count · quote · before/after path>
```
