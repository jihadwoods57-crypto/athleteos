## 1. Who this is for
AthleteOS is an accountability platform for athletes and the people who support them
(parents/guardians, personal trainers, sports-performance coaches, nutritionists, high
school and college coaches). **Some athletes are minors.** We treat all athlete data as
sensitive and apply heightened protections to minors' data (see §7).

## 2. What we collect
- **Account/identity:** name, email, role, sport, position, school/program, training
  frequency, age (collected at setup).
- **Health & nutrition data:** logged meals and their macros, meal **photos**, daily
  accountability scores, body weight and weight goals, hydration, weekly check-in
  answers (energy, recovery, sleep, soreness, motivation), and any wearable recovery
  data you choose to connect.
- **Relationships:** the coaches, trainers, nutritionists, and guardians you link to,
  and (for staff accounts) the athletes on your roster/book.
- **Messages** you send to linked people through the app.
- **Device/technical:** local app storage for offline use; standard service logs when
  the backend is enabled. We do **not** use third-party advertising or cross-app
  tracking SDKs, and we do not show the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

## 3. How we use it
To provide the core service: analyze meals, compute your score, show your trends, let
your linked coach/parent see what you have agreed to share, generate weekly reports, and
send the reminders you enable. We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 4. Meal-photo analysis by a third-party AI
When meal-photo analysis is enabled, the photo is sent to our AI provider,
**Anthropic (Claude)**, to estimate the foods and macros. The photo is processed for
that purpose and is not used by us for advertising. [Confirm Anthropic's data-handling

## 5. Who can see your data
- **You** always can.
- **People you link** (a coach, trainer, nutritionist, or guardian) can see the specific
  data you consent to share (e.g., daily score, compliance, weight trend). You can stop
  sharing at any time.
- **Service providers** that run our infrastructure ([Supabase] for database/storage;
  Anthropic for meal analysis) under contract.
- We disclose data if required by law.

backend use. A parent/guardian can review their child's data, request its deletion, and
withdraw consent at any time by contacting support@athleteos.app or using the in-app
controls. [Describe the exact verifiable-consent method used — e.g., consent via a

## 9. Your rights (access, deletion, export, portability)
You can **export a copy of your data** and **permanently delete your account and data**
from the app's Account screen at any time. EU/UK (GDPR) and California (CCPA/CPRA)
residents have additional rights (access, correction, deletion, portability, and to opt
out of sale/share — we do not sell or share). To exercise these, use the in-app controls
or email support@athleteos.app.
