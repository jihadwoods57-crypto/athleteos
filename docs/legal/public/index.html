How it works

One photo. One honest number.

01 / Snap the plate

Photograph the meal

One photo. OnStandard reads the plate, estimates the macros, and scores it for the athlete's goal — no searching, no guesswork.

02 / Get the score

See where you stand

A daily Execution Score, A to F. It moves the moment you do the work — not before. The number reflects the day you actually had.

03 / The coach sees it

Show up, visibly

Link a coach, trainer, or parent. They see the day you agreed to share and who on the roster is slipping — before it becomes a habit.

The number reflects what you did.
Not what you meant to do.
